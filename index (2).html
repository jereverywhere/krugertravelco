About

I'm Jeremy Kruger.

My passion for travel led me to moving abroad full time, living and working remotely in places like Mexico, Indonesia, Croatia, and Brazil. After seeing firsthand how life-changing travel can be, I started using that experience to help others travel the same way.

My experience spending years abroad shapes how I approach every trip I plan. Real on-the-ground knowledge, genuine local connections, and an understanding of what it actually means to travel somewhere rather than just visit it. I like to put together the kind of trip where you come home feeling like you actually got to know a place.

That time living abroad translates directly into the kind of access most advisors can't offer. The best taco stands in Mexico City that don't exist on the internet. Secret beaches in Albania before the crowds find them. A Balinese guide who will bring you to his family home. Connections in cities across the world who send me their current favorite spots every time a client heads their way. Years of actually living somewhere builds a different kind of knowledge than two weeks of visiting it — and that's not something you can google.

05
Price Monitoring

I use a rate-tracking system that monitors refundable hotel bookings and alerts me when a price drops. When it does, I rebook at the lower rate. You pay less without having to think about it.

06
Groups, Weddings & Retreats

Corporate retreats, weddings, multi-family vacations. I negotiate room blocks, review and manage contracts, source meeting and event space, and coordinate the countless logistical details.
